Stephanie Jane Edwards

About

Stephanie Jane Edwards is a life-long learner. She has a curiosity to understand how things work, and a desire to learn in order to apply skills directly toward making the world a better place. Stephanie Jane can mine and analyze data in maps and databases, using the findings to inform decisions and collaborative community projects. She is only just beginning to learn web development to build tools and data solutions on the internet for community development. There is so much to learn!
